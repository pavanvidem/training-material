1. Click on History options {% icon galaxy-history-options %}
2. Select **Export history to file**
3. Select the format. RO-crate or compressed TGZ file.

   ![history export format selection menu]({% link topics/introduction/images/galaxy-intro-rdm/history-export.png %})

4. Select a destination. Options include:
   - Temporary Direct Download (will generate a download link valid for 24 hours)
   - Zenodo
   - One of your [configured repositories]({% link faqs/galaxy/manage_your_repositories.md %})

   ![history export format selection menu]({% link topics/introduction/images/galaxy-intro-rdm/history-export-destination.png %})

5. Configure your chosen option and export.
